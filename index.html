Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

The founding father of the Republic of Turkey

A memory from a school visit

Atatürk is a superior personality, a great commander and statesman who is rarely found in history who won the love of humanity. Atatürk said, “To see me does not necessarily mean to see my face. If you understand and feel my ideas, my feelings, that is enough ”and“ If I die, I am sure that our noble nation will never leave the path we are walking with, I feel comfortable with it! ” He showed his trust in the Turkish nation with his words. The Turkish nation has commemorated and continues to remember Atatürk, whose value he understands more and more every day, in accordance with his greatness.

19 May 2020 is the 139th anniversary of Atatürk's birth. Atatürk have agreed May 19th as his birthday, which is also the beginning of the process to the establishment of the Republic of Turkey. In this respect, 19 May is engraved in the memories as a day that reflects Atatürk's identification with his nation in the best way. The Turkish Nation celebrated the birth of Atatürk with various activities, and showed and shows their devotion with a great affection and respect.
